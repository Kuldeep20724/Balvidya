👨‍💻 About the App

BalVidya is a colorful, interactive learning platform for children to explore alphabets, numbers, animals, rhymes, stories, and quizzes. Designed to be 100% offline-capable and mobile-first, it's made using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and AJAX. No login needed – just pure learning fun!

🧑‍🎓 About the Creator

This app is created by Kuldeep Saini, a passionate developer and educator from India. The goal is to make learning joyful and accessible to all children without requiring an internet connection or complex setup.
